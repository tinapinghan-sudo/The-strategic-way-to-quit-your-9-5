The intentional roadmap to leave your 9-5

Quitting your 9-5 shouldn't be a leap of faith.
It should be a strategy.

Hi, I'm Tina. After years as a tech professional executing major product launches, I realized the most important launch I would ever plan was my own exit.

Before that, I spent three years going through the motions in a 9-5 that looked great on paper but left me completely uninspired. I stayed far too long because I was stuck overanalyzing two questions: What if I can't find something better? What if I run out of money?

When I finally mapped out my departure, I learned two things: I was far more ready than I thought, and I almost left more than $13,000 on the table simply by picking the wrong final calendar date.

So, I built the exact framework I desperately needed. This isn't a vague guide on "how to quit" (the resignation letter is the easy part). It is an operational strategy designed to ensure you claim every single penny you worked for, keep the door back to corporate open, and step confidently into your next chapter.
